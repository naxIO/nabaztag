To find your weather code do the following:

1. Browse to weather.yahoo.com
2. Enter your city in the search box:

3. Click go.
4. Click on your city.
5. Once your forecast is displayed, write down the numbers at the end of the URL.

Example: The URL for London is
http://weather.yahoo.com/england/greater-london/london-44418/

The weather code is 44418

Click your browser's back button to go back.
